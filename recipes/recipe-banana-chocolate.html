Odin Recipes

Ingredients

  • 1 slice bread
  • 1 egg
  • 2 tablespoons milk
  • 1 teaspoon cocoa powder
  • banana, sliced
  • chocolate chip, to taste
TOPPING
  • whipped cream, to taste
  • chocolate syrup, to taste

Preparation

  1. Slice bread into cubes.
  2. In a bowl, add egg, milk, and cocoa powder, and mix.
  3. In a microwave-safe mug, place half of the cubed bread and desired amount of bananas and chocolate chips.
  4. Place the rest of the bread and fix-ins and pour the egg mixture into the mug.
  5. Microwave for 1½ minutes or until egg is fully cooked.
  6. Top it off with whipped cream and chocolate syrup.
  7. Enjoy!
